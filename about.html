About NIROG

Committed to Better and Accessible Healthcare

NIROG Diagnostics is a trusted diagnostic service provider in Bhubaneswar, committed to making quality healthcare testing simple, affordable and accessible for every family.

We provide a wide range of pathology tests, preventive health packages and convenient home sample collection services through trusted diagnostic laboratory partners.

With trained professionals, hygienic sample collection practices and reliable laboratory processes, NIROG Diagnostics aims to help patients make informed decisions about their health with confidence.
